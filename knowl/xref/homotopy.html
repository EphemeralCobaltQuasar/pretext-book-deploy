Formally, a homotopy between two continuous functions \(f\) and \(g\) from a topological space \(X\) to a topological space \(Y\) is defined to be a continuous function \(H : X \times [0,1] \to Y\) from the product of the space \(X\) with the unit interval \([0,1]\) to \(Y\) such that \(H(x,0) = f(x)\) and \(H(x,1) = g(x)\) for all \(x \in X\text{.}\) wikipedia
