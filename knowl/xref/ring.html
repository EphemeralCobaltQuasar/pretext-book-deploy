Definition 1.1.15. ring.

A group R with two binary operation, addition + and multiplication \(\cdot\text{.}\) R is an abelian group under addition and a monoid under multiplication. Multiplication is distributive with respect to addition,
\begin{equation*} a \cdot (b + c) = a \cdot b + a \cdot c \; \text{(left distributivity)} \end{equation*}
