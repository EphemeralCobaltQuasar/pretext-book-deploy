Definition 1.2.48. change of basepoint.

Either way, we define a change-of-basepoint map \(\beta_h : \pi_1(X, x_1) \to \pi_1(X, x_0)\) by \(\beta_h[f] = [h \cdot f \cdot \bar{h}]\text{,}\) homotopy class. This is well-defined since if \(f_t\) is a homotopy of loops based at \(x_1\text{,}\) then \(h \cdot f_t \cdot \bar{h}\) is a homotopy of loops based at \(x_0\text{.}\)
