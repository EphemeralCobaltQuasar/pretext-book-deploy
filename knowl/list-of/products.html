Definition 1.2.28. products.

If \(X\) and \(Y\) are cell complex, then \(X \times Y\) has the structure of a cell complex with n-cell the products \(e^m_\alpha \times e^n_\beta\) where \(e^m_\alpha\) ranges over the n-cell of \(X\) and \(e^n_\beta\) ranges over the n-cell of \(Y\text{.}\)
