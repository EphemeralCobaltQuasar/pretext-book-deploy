Definition 1.2.68. group action.

Given a group \(G\) and a space \(Y\text{,}\) then an action of \(G\) on \(Y\) is a homomorphism \(\rho\) from \(G\) to the group \(\text{Homeo}(Y)\) of all homeomorphisms from \(Y\) to itself. Thus to each \(g \in G\) is associated a homeomorphism \(\rho(g) : Y \to Y\text{,}\)
