Definition 1.2.6. mapping cone.

Closely related to the mapping cylinder \(M_{f}\) is the mapping cone \(C_{f} = Y \amalg_{f} CX\text{,}\) where \(CX\) is the cone \((X \times I) / (X \times \{ 0 \})\text{.}\) We attach this to \(Y\) along \(X \times \{ 1 \}\) via the identifications \((x,1) \sim f(x)\text{.}\)
