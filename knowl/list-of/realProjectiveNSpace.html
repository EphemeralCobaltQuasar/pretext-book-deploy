Definition 1.2.23. real projective n space.

Real projective n-space \(\mathbb{R}P^n\) is defined to be the space of all lines through the origin in \(\mathbb{R}^{n+1}\text{.}\) Each such line is determined by a nonzero vector in \(\mathbb{R}^{n+1}\text{,}\) unique up to scalar multiplication, and \(\mathbb{R}P^n\) is topologized as the quotient space of \(\mathbb{R}^{n+1} - \{0\}\) under the equivalence relation \(v \sim \lambda v\) for scalars \(\lambda \neq 0\text{.}\)
