Definition 1.2.29. quotients.

for X/A, compress A to a point. leave everything else in X the same. Quotients. If \((X, A)\) is a CW pair consisting of a cell complex \(X\) and a subcomplex \(A\text{,}\) then the quotients space \(X / A\) inherits a natural cell complex structure from \(X\text{.}\) The cells of \(X / A\) are the cells of \(X - A\) plus one new 0-n-cell, the image of \(A\) in \(X / A\text{.}\) For a n-cell \(e^n_\alpha\) of \(X - A\) attached by \(\varphi_\alpha : S^{n-1} \to X^{n-1}\text{,}\) the attaching map for the corresponding n-cell in \(X / A\) is the composition \(S^{n-1} \to X^{n-1} \to X^{n-1} / A^{n-1}\text{.}\)
