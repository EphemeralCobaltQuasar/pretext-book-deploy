Definition 1.2.33. Wedge Sum.

\(X \sqcup Y\text{.}\) Given spaces \(X\) and \(Y\) with chosen points \(x_0 \in X\) and \(y_0 \in Y\text{,}\) the wedge sum \(X \vee Y\) is the quotient of the disjoint union \(X \sqcup Y\) obtained by identifying \(x_0\) and \(y_0\) to a single point.
