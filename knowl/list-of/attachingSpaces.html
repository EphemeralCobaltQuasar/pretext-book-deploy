We start with a space \(X_{0}\) and another space \(X_{1}\) that we wish to attach to \(X_{0}\) by identifying the points in a subspace \(A \subset X_{1}\) with points of \(X_{0}\text{.}\) xre The data needed to do this is a map \(f : A \to X_{0}\text{,}\) for then we can form a quotient space of \(X_{0} \amalg X_{1}\) by identifying each point \(a \in A\) with its image \(f(a) \in X_{0}\text{.}\) We denote this quotient space by \(X_{0} \amalg_{f} X_{1}\text{,}\) the space \(X_{0}\) with \(X_{1}\) attached along A via f. When \((X_{1}, A) = (D^{n}, S^{n-1})\) we have the case of attaching an \(n\)-cell to \(X_{0}\) via a map \(f : S^{\,n-1} \to X_{0}\text{.}\)
